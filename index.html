Privacy Policy - Word Hunt

Effective Date: December 31, 2024

Welcome to Word Hunt! This privacy policy outlines how idevali handles information in relation to our word game application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure gaming experience.

1. Information Collection

Word Hunt is designed to respect your privacy. We operate with minimal data collection:

  • Game Progress: The app stores your game progress and scores locally on your device only.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, email addresses, or location data.
  • No Analytics: We do not use tracking or analytics tools to monitor user behavior.

2. App Permissions

Word Hunt requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage Access: To save your game progress locally on your device
  • Internet Access: For app updates through the Google Play Store

3. Advertisement Policy

Our app does not display any third-party advertisements or use ad-tracking technologies.

4. Children's Privacy

Word Hunt is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

5. Data Storage and Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. Since we don't collect personal data, there is no risk of personal data breaches on our end.

6. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our website. Continued use of Word Hunt after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Android App Distribution

Word Hunt is distributed through the Google Play Store and follows all Google Play's developer policies and guidelines.
